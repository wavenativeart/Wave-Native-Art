About Me

Kayli Marchese

As for many, art is an expression and outlet where the imagination is brought into reality. While this is also true for myself, my everything is put into my pieces. Hours of sadness, anger, and love are worked through my canvas. Being born and raised in Jupiter, Florida, I’ve felt bound to the water, it makes me feel free and weightless in the big unknown. I paint what I love and love what I paint, between the ocean landscapes or the creatures it homes, I always turn to nature as my muse. Painting is my passion and my heart, a dream I’ve held since I could remember. I will continue to live out my dream of being an artist until my hands can no longer hold onto a brush, but my art will always remain as small pieces of me and my life and I’m honored people get to enjoy it along with me.
